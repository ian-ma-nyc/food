Baked Salmon

Description

Baked salmon recipe.

Ingredients

  • Wild Salmon
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Turn on oven. Bake - 450 degrees. Preheat for 20 minutes.
  2. Put lemon juice on salmon. Do not salt and pepper until right before salmon is put into oven.
  3. Cook for 15 minutes.
