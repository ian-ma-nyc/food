Chicken Recipe

Description

This is Ian's famous grilled chicken recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat pan!!!
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  3. Add olive oil to pan and wait until hot.
  4. Add chicken seasoned side down. Change heat to medium. Season top with salt and pepper. Wait 5 minutes.
  5. Flip sides and wait another 5 minutes.
