Chicken Breast

Description

Grilled chicken breast recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Use tenderizer on chicken.
  2. Heat pan.
  3. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  4. Add olive oil to pan and wait until hot.
  5. Add chicken seasoned side down. Change heat to medium. Season top with salt and pepper. Wait 3 minutes.
  6. Flip sides and wait another 3 minutes.
