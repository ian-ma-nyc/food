Seared Crispy Salmon

Description

Seared crispy salmon recipe.

Ingredients

  • Wild Salmon
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat pan.
  2. Add oilive oil to pan and wait until hot.
  3. Roll salmon and add cuts to the skin. Sprinkle salt into the cuts.
  4. Add salmon skin down onto pan. Change to medium heat. Wait 4-5 minutes. Season top of salmon with salt and pepper.
  5. Flip salmon so skin side is up. Wait 3 minutes.
  6. Flip salmon skin side down again. Shut off fire. Let it rest.
