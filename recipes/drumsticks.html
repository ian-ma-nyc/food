Chicken Drumsticks

Description

Baked chicken drumsticks recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Drumsticks (8 pieces)
  • Olive Oil (2 Tbsp)
  • Seat Salt (1 Tsp)
  • Black Pepper (0.5 Tsp)
  • Garlic Powder (1 Tsp)
  • Smoked Paprika (1.5 Tsp)
  • Chili Powder (0.25 Tsp)
  • Onion Powder (1 Tsp)

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bag and let sit.
  2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  3. Bake for 40-45 minutes.
